What is .agt?

.agt is a top-level domain (TLD) that stands for "agent". .agt domains are specifically designed for AI agents, autonomous systems, and agent platforms, creating a dedicated namespace for the agentic web.

Where can I register .agt domains?

.agt domains can be registered at Freename.io. Simply search for your desired .agt domain name, complete the purchase, and choose your preferred blockchain for minting. Visit https://freename.io/discover/agt to start.

Why is .agt the best domain for AI agents?

.agt stands for "agent", making it the natural and intuitive choice for AI agents and autonomous systems. It creates instant recognition that a service or system is agent-focused, helping establish trust and clarity in agent-to-agent and human-to-agent interactions.

What types of AI agents should use .agt domains?

Any AI agent or autonomous system benefits from a .agt domain - from personal AI assistants and research agents to trading bots and customer service agents. Agent platforms, marketplaces, and infrastructure providers also use .agt to signal their focus on the agentic ecosystem.

How does agent-to-agent communication work with .agt?

As AI agents become more autonomous, they need standardized ways to discover and communicate with each other. .agt domains provide a consistent namespace where agents can be found, verified, and contacted, enabling the emerging agent economy.

Can I register multiple .agt domains for different agents?

Yes! Many organizations register multiple .agt domains for their various AI agents and services. For example, you might have support.agt for customer service, research.agt for analysis, and your company name.agt as your main agent platform.

What blockchain networks are available?

When minting your .agt domain, you can choose from several blockchain networks including Polygon, Solana, BNBChain, BASE, Aurora, and others. Each offers different benefits in terms of transaction costs and ecosystem compatibility.

How do I choose a good agent domain name?

Think about your agent's primary function or identity. Descriptive names like assistant.agt or research.agt work well, as do brandable names. The key is choosing something memorable that clearly represents your agent's purpose in the agentic ecosystem.
